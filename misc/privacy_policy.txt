Privacy Policy — CIDR Match — IP Range Checker

This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.
There are no analytics, no telemetry, and no external servers operated by this
extension.

To show the table of connection information, the extension observes the IP
addresses your browser connects to as you load pages. All such information is
kept in RAM on your computer and is never transmitted over the network by the
extension.

Any CIDR ruleset you paste into the options page is stored in
chrome.storage.local on your own device only. It is not synced, uploaded, or
sent anywhere.

Technically there is one exception: if you use the optional "Look up" context
menu feature (inherited from IPvFoo), your browser will navigate to a URL
managed by a third party (e.g. Hurricane Electric / bgp.he.net), containing the
selected domain name or IP address. This only happens when you explicitly
choose it.

This extension is a derivative work based on IPvFoo (https://github.com/pmarks-net/ipvfoo).
